Privacy Policy

Numingo Mobile Application
Last updated: December 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Olcay Güneş ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Numingo mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using Numingo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we collect:

  • Username (chosen by you)
  • Email address
  • Profile avatar selection
  • Authentication data from third-party sign-in providers (Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In)

2.2 Game Data

We collect gameplay information to provide the multiplayer experience:

  • Game statistics (wins, losses, average attempts, win streaks)
  • Match history and scores
  • Elo rating and leaderboard rankings
  • Friend list and friend requests
  • Game invitations sent and received

2.3 Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Push notification tokens (for game invites and friend requests)
  • App version and settings preferences
  • General location (country/region, derived from IP address)
  • Device identifiers for analytics and advertising purposes

2.4 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the App:

  • Features used and actions taken within the App
  • Session duration and frequency of use
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Error logs for troubleshooting

3. Third-Party Services

Numingo uses the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

3.1 Supabase (Backend Services)

We use Supabase for authentication, database storage, and real-time features. Your account information, game data, and multiplayer data are stored on Supabase servers located in the European Union.

Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

3.2 Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. This service collects usage data, device information, and app performance metrics to help us improve the user experience.

Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3.3 Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix technical issues. This service automatically collects crash reports, including device state, stack traces, and diagnostic information when the App encounters an error.

Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3.4 Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send push notifications for game invites, friend requests, match updates, and other game-related alerts.

Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3.5 Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID), IP address, and usage data to serve advertisements, including personalized ads based on your interests.

Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings or through the Ad Choices option when available.

3.6 Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, we receive basic profile information (name, email address) as authorized by you during the sign-in process. We do not receive your password or have access to your Google/Apple account.

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14.5 and later, we will request your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before collecting your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) for personalized advertising purposes. You can change this permission at any time in your device's Settings under Privacy > Tracking.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the game service
  • To create and manage your account
  • To enable multiplayer features, matchmaking, and friend connections
  • To send push notifications about game events (invites, match results, friend requests)
  • To display leaderboards and rankings
  • To display advertisements
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the App
  • To identify and fix bugs and technical issues
  • To prevent fraud and ensure fair gameplay
  • To comply with legal obligations

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information.

Security measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Row Level Security (RLS) policies on all database tables
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security assessments

7. Data Retention

We retain your account and game data as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain certain information for legal or legitimate business purposes.

Analytics and crash data is retained according to the retention policies of our third-party service providers (typically 14 months for Firebase Analytics).

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. Our primary database is hosted in the European Union (Supabase). Firebase and AdMob services may process data in the United States and other countries.

When we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Children's Privacy

Numingo is rated for users aged 4 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area) without verifiable parental consent.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to delete such information from our servers.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Additional Controls

  • Opt out of personalized advertisements in your device settings
  • Disable push notifications in the App settings or device settings
  • Delete your account through the App's profile settings

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
