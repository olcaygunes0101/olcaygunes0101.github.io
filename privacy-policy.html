Privacy Policy for Numingo

Last updated: December 15, 2025

Introduction

Olcay Güneş ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Numingo mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we collect:

  • Username (chosen by you)
  • Email address
  • Profile avatar selection
  • Authentication data from third-party sign-in providers (Google, Apple)

Game Data

We collect gameplay information to provide the multiplayer experience:

  • Game statistics (wins, losses, average attempts)
  • Match history and scores
  • Elo rating and leaderboard rankings
  • Friend list and game invitations

Device Information

We may collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Push notification tokens (for game invites and friend requests)
  • App preferences and settings

Third-Party Services

Numingo uses the following third-party services:

Supabase

We use Supabase for backend services including authentication, database, and real-time features. Your account and game data is stored on Supabase servers.

Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send push notifications for game invites, friend requests, and other game-related alerts.

Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads.

Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, we receive basic profile information (name, email) as authorized by you during the sign-in process.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game service
  • To enable multiplayer features and matchmaking
  • To send push notifications about game events
  • To display leaderboards and rankings
  • To manage friend lists and game invitations
  • To display advertisements
  • To improve and optimize the app

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your information.

Data Retention

We retain your account and game data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Numingo is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertisements
  • Disable push notifications in app settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
