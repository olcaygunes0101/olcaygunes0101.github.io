Introduction

Numingo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

Important Note: We do not collect or store any personal data ourselves. However, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect certain information for ad personalization.

Data Collection

Information We Collect:

We do not directly collect, store, or share any personal data. Numingo is a simple number guessing game where all your game statistics and progress are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

Information Collected by Third Parties:

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect and process certain information including:

  • Device advertising ID (IDFA on iOS)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage data for ad performance
  • General location information (country/region level)

This information is used to provide personalized advertisements and improve ad relevance. For more information about how Google uses this data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Information Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game statistics (games played, win rate, average attempts)
  • Game history and scores
  • App preferences and settings

This information never leaves your device and is only accessible by you through the app.

Third-Party Services

Numingo integrates with the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements within the app. AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Google's Advertising Policies.

Advertising

We display advertisements through Google AdMob, which may show:

  • Interstitial ads (full-screen ads between games)
  • Rewarded video ads (optional ads for in-game benefits)

You can limit ad personalization through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Tracking ‚Üí Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • iOS: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Apple Advertising ‚Üí Disable "Personalized Ads"

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no privacy concerns for children using our app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
